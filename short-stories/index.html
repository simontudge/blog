Simon's Short Stories

Collection of Short Stories

The Magic Cave

Fourteen year old Kiera is frustrated with the old men in her argumentative little village. She wanders off into the nearby mountains where she discovers a magic cave that whispers back to her in her own voice.

The Farm

A retelling of the biblical story of Adam and Eve, imagining humanity's descent from paradise in a new light.

Together

A young man leaves his troubles behind to hike in the wilderness only to find that cataclysmic events unfold in the world whilst he is disconnected. A musing on love, loneliness, pain and war.
